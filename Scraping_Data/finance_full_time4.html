Lemonade3.6
Office Operations Lead (Site Manager)
Tel Aviv-Yafo

We're looking for a big-hearted Office Operations Lead to manage our Office Ops team and oversee our vibrant and growing TLV site.

You'll oversee the talented team that manages ongoing daily operations and logistics, facilities, events planning, and office life for over 300 employees in the Tel Aviv office. Your day-to-day will involve planning and executing large-scale, company-wide initiatives: driving strategy, working with internal and external stakeholders, building budgets, communicating and implementing plans, and collecting feedback in order to share recommendations with leadership. We're looking for a person who shines in the role of a "generalist", is resourceful and creative, and has great project management and managerial skills. We specifically desire someone experienced in taking a "bird's eye view" of Office Operations, while maintaining a "pixel-perfect" approach to every aspect of their planning and execution.

This people person should be detail oriented, customer-obsessed, and ready to lead the team who manages ongoing daily operations and logistics, facilities, events planning, and office life for our +300 TLV Makers.


In this role you'll

  • Oversee all office operations projects and processes
  • Collaborates closely with other teams to manage processes that have a broad impact on our employees' experience, ensuring we provide a best in-class, delightful, and unique workplace experience
  • Manage the local Office Operations team
  • Partner with different support functions such as People Ops, Finance, Legal, IT and Talent Acquisition to create a delightful, compliant and unique employee experience
  • Create, implement, and monitor company and site policies

What you'll need

  • 8+ years of operations and people management experience
  • Experience in hyper-growth organizations with 300+ employees
  • An extraordinary communicator, with fantastic verbal and written communication skills in both Hebrew and English
  • Broad knowledge across various employee experience-related fields
  • Ability to learn on the job, stay organized, adapt, and approach challenges with endless creativity and a "can-solve-any-problem" attitude An execution state of mind, with no hesitation to take on hands-on work and drive the team to deliver faster
  • Superior attention to details (while always seeing the big picture) and an enthusiasm for delighting employees
  • Exceptional EQ - natural ability to 'read' and influence people
  • Superior project management skills
